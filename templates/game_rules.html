ROUTE INFO

Budget and Outcomes

You begin with $2. Choosing to invest costs $0.5.

  • If you invest and the project succeeds, you will earn an additional $0.5, for a total of $2.5.
  • If you invest and the project fails, you will lose the $0.5 you invested, for a total of $1.5.
  • If you do not invest, you will keep your $2.

Private Information

You will observe one ball drawn from a jar containing 100 balls. One color is in the majority, but you do not know which one.

Public Information

In Phase 1, you will see previous participants’ decisions and their position in the sequence. In Phase 2, you will also see confidence information.

Decision Process

  • Phase 1: Decide whether to invest and report your confidence level.
  • Phase 2: See added confidence information and make a final decision.
  • You may keep your original choice or change it.

Project Success

The project succeeds if the majority color in the jar is blue AND the number of investors reaches the required threshold.

Project Failure

The project fails if the majority color in the jar is red, OR if the number of investors is below the required threshold.

At any stage, you may click the Mission Brief button to view these rules again.
